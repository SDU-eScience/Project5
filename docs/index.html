Overall Goals and Scope¬∂

In Fall 2020, a consortium formed by the Technical University of Denmark (DTU), Aarhus University (AU) and the University of Southern Denmark (SDU) was approved by the DeiC board for the realization of the DeiC ‚ÄúProject 5‚Äù for the creation of the National Integration Portal system. The purpose of this software project is to drive the development of a shared self-service and dashboard with integration into the national HPC centers and international HPC facilities like the pan-European pre-exascale supercomputer, LUMI. The consortium proposal was to expand UCloud with the necessary new functionality.
